Welcome

A thrilling top-down zombie shooter with a horror twist.

About the Game

"Welcome" is a 3D top-down zombie shooter developed by JADU GameStudio. Your objective is to survive as long as possible while scoring high. Set in a dark and eerie atmosphere, the game challenges you to manage your resources and fight relentless zombie hordes.

Key Features

  • Dark and immersive horror atmosphere
  • Resource management for survival
  • Two game modes: "Last Stand" & "Zombie Rush"
  • Two difficulty levels: Easy & Hard
  • Intense zombie combat with increasing difficulty
